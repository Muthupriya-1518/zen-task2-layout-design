About us:

Meesho is an Indian social commerce platform that enables individuals to start their own online businesses by reselling products through social media channels like WhatsApp, Facebook, and Instagram. Founded in 2015, Meesho allows users to curate a selection of products from various suppliers and then share these with their networks, earning a commission on each sale.

Meesho has been growing rapidly and has become an essential platform for businesses in India and around the world. With over 10 million users, Meesho offers a wide range of products and services, from fashion to electronics, home appliances, and more.

Key Features of Meesho:

  1. Social Selling: Meesho allows users to sell products directly through social media platforms, making it easy for individuals to reach their customers.
  2. Wide Product Range: The platform offers a diverse range of products, including clothing, accessories, home decor, and beauty items.
  3. Zero Investment: Users can start selling without any upfront costs. They can select products to sell and only pay for them once they receive orders.
  4. User-Friendly App: Meesho provides an easy-to-use mobile application that helps users manage their inventory, orders, and payments seamlessly.
  5. Training and Support: The platform offers training resources and support to help resellers improve their selling techniques and grow their businesses.
  6. Payment and Logistics: Meesho handles payments and logistics, making it easier for resellers to focus on marketing and selling.
